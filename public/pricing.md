# Pricing — FYPro

FYPro uses one-time payments (no subscriptions). All prices are in Nigerian Naira (NGN). Payments processed via Paystack.

## Free

- Price: ₦0 — free forever
- Features:
  - Topic Validator (unlimited)
  - Chapter Architect (3 free runs)
  - Methodology Advisor (3 free runs)
  - Defence Simulator free trial (3 questions)
- Limits: Limited runs on AI-heavy steps; no Writing Planner, no Project Reviewer, no full Defence Simulator

## Student Plan

- Price: ₦2,000 one-time per project
- Features:
  - Everything in Free
  - Writing Planner (week-by-week schedule based on defence date)
  - Project Reviewer (10 runs — AI review of uploaded PDF drafts)
  - Supervisor Meeting Prep Agent
- Limits: Does not include Defence Simulator or Defence Certificates
- Upgrade path: Pay ₦1,500 more to reach Defense Plan (total ₦3,500)

## Express Defence

- Price: ₦2,000 one-time
- Who it is for: Students who already have a completed draft and only need defence preparation
- Features:
  - Project Reviewer — upload PDF for AI review
  - Defence Brief — AI-generated preparation document (opening statement, model answers for weak spots, examiner Q&A)
  - Defence Simulator — 3-AI-examiner simulation with scoring and defence certificate
- Note: This is a separate product from the main 6-step workflow. Express users get their own isolated dashboard.

## Defense Plan

- Price: ₦3,500 one-time (or ₦1,500 upgrade from Student Plan)
- Features:
  - Everything in Student Plan
  - Full Defence Simulator (3 AI examiners — The Methodologist, The Subject Expert, The External Examiner)
  - Defence Certificate (PDF, downloadable, with QR verification — unlocked at score 7/10+)
  - Project Reviewer (unlimited)
  - Red Flag Scanner
- Limits: One project per payment

## Project Reset (Add-on)

- Price: ₦1,500 one-time add-on
- What it does: Allows starting a new project without losing the existing one
- Available to: All paid plan users
- Note: Consumable — used once per purchase
