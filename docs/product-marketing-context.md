# Product Marketing Context — FYPro

*Last updated: 2026-05-06*

## Product Overview
**One-liner:** The supervisor most final year students never had.
**What it does:** FYPro is an AI-powered research companion that guides Nigerian university final year students through a structured 6-step workflow — from validating a rough topic idea to simulating a full three-examiner defense panel. It fills the gap left by absent or unresponsive supervisors, giving students the structure, feedback, and pressure-testing they would otherwise never receive.
**Product category:** Academic research tool / FYP assistant / AI study companion
**Product type:** SaaS — freemium with one-time per-project payments
**Business model:**
- Free: Topic Validator (3 runs), basic Chapter Architect, Methodology recommendation, Writing Planner (4 weeks only)
- Student Pack: ₦2,000 one-time — full workflow access
- Defense Pack: ₦3,500 one-time — everything + Defense Simulator (5 sessions)
- Project Reset: ₦1,500 — fresh start on same account

## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:** Nigerian final year students have supervisors who are unavailable, unresponsive, or who provide feedback too late to act on. Students are expected to produce a complete research project with almost no structured guidance.

**Why alternatives fall short:**
- ChatGPT: Generic, blank-box responses with no knowledge of their faculty, department, or Nigerian university system. Doesn't interrogate them. Forgets context between sessions.
- YouTube tutorials: Passive, general, not topic-specific
- Older students / seniors: Unreliable, inconsistent, often share their own flawed projects
- Hiring a consultant: Expensive (₦20,000–₦80,000), often writes the project for them — student still fails the defense because they can't answer questions about work they didn't do

**What it costs them:**
- Time: Students spend 3+ months on topics that are not researchable at their level
- Money: Paying consultants ₦20,000–₦80,000 to write projects they cannot defend
- The year: Failing a defense even when the written project is good

**Emotional tension:**
- Panic: "My defense is in 3 weeks and I still don't understand my methodology"
- Shame: "My supervisor hasn't read a single chapter — I can't even ask questions"
- Fear: "I'm going to freeze when they ask me something I haven't prepared for"
- Regret: "I wish this existed when I started, not when I was already in a panic"

**The insurance framing — critical for conversion:**
Students are not paying for a tool. They are paying to stop losing. The defense is coming whether they are ready or not. ₦2,000 is less than one textbook, less than two weeks of data, less than one consultation with a project helper. The real question is not "why would I pay ₦2,000?" — it is "what is failing my defense worth to me?" Position FYPro as insurance, not as software.

## Competitive Landscape
**Direct:** No known direct competitor in Nigeria offering a structured, multi-step FYP companion with defense simulation.

**Secondary (same problem, different solution):**
- ChatGPT / Gemini — falls short because it has no knowledge of Nigerian faculties, no structured workflow, no defense simulation, forgets context, and gives the same answer to everyone
- Academic consulting services — falls short because they write for you (you still fail the defense), they're expensive, and they create dependency

**Indirect (conflicting approach):**
- Asking older students / coursemates — falls short because advice is anecdotal, often based on outdated or wrong approaches, and has no accountability
- Doing nothing / hoping for the best — the default for most students

**One-sentence competitive position:** ChatGPT is a blank box. FYPro is a structured journey that knows who you are.

## Differentiation
**Key differentiators:**
- The only tool that knows the student's faculty, department, methodology, chapter structure, and deadline — and uses all of it in every response
- The only tool with a three-examiner defense simulation that adapts to the student's actual project weaknesses
- Built specifically for the Nigerian university system (faculty structures, academic calendar, public holidays, exam periods)
- Structured 6-step journey — not a blank chatbot. Every step builds on the last. The student never has to know what question to ask next.
- Context continuity across steps — the writing plan knows the methodology; the reviewer knows the chapter structure; nothing contradicts
- Does not write the project for the student — prepares them to own and defend it

**How we do it differently:** Context-aware, sequential AI guidance. Each step passes its outputs into the next step so the AI never contradicts earlier decisions. The Defense Simulator uses the student's actual red flags, not generic questions.

**Why that's better:** Students walk out of FYPro able to defend every decision in their project. They understand their own work. They're not holding a document a consultant wrote — they're holding work they built and can answer for.

**Why students choose FYPro over alternatives:**
- It's structured — they don't have to know what question to ask next
- It knows Nigerian universities — not generic Western academic advice
- The Defense Simulator is something no other tool offers
- It's priced for students — ₦2,000 is less than most students spend on data in a week

## Switching Dynamics

- Supervisor has not responded in weeks or months
- Defense date announced and they feel unprepared
- Wasted months on a topic a supervisor finally rejected

## Goals
**Business goal:** Reach paying users at launch (June 12, 2026) and validate the ₦2,000 Student Pack conversion funnel before scaling to other Nigerian universities.

**Conversion action:** The primary funnel is:
1. Student arrives at landing page (social, word of mouth, search)
2. Runs Topic Validator free — **no account required** — on their actual topic
3. Sees a sharp, honest verdict that surprises them → trust is established
4. Creates account to save progress and continue to Step 2
5. Hits paywall at Step 4 (Writing Planner) or Step 5 (Project Reviewer) → upgrades to Student Pack (₦2,000)
6. Defense date approaches → upgrades to Defense Pack (₦3,500)

**The free trial moment is the most important conversion mechanic:** Step 1 (Topic Validator) must work with no signup and no payment. The product earns trust by doing something useful on the student's actual topic before asking for anything. This is the primary trust-builder pre-launch.

**Pre-launch proof needed:**
- A "before / after" example showing a rough topic becoming sharp in under 2 minutes
- A student count or waitlist number to anchor social proof
- One shareable moment from the Defense Simulator that demonstrates what students are missing

**Current metrics:** Pre-launch. No live conversion data yet.
**v3 target:** B2B institutional model — universities and departments pay a flat semester fee for all final year students.
