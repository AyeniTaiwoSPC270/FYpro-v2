# FYPro — Marketing Week 1

**Start date:** 2026-06-22  
**Goal:** First 5 real users + 3 course reps contacted + 1 Twitter thread live

---

## Job 1 — Get First 5 Users (Days 1–2)

**Who:** Sister + 3 roommates + 1–2 others you know personally  
**What to offer:** Free Express Defence access (grant via `/admin/health`)  
**Why Express Defence:** They're almost done with their projects — they need defense prep, not the full workflow

**WhatsApp message to send (one by one, not broadcast):**
> "I built a defense prep tool — AI plays 3 hostile examiners and grills you on your project before the real thing. I'm giving you free access because I need real feedback from people who are actually about to defend. Sign up at fypro.com.ng and send me your email."

**After they sign up:**
1. Go to `fypro.com.ng/admin/health`
2. Find their email → grant Express Defence entitlement
3. Follow up the next day to ask what they think

**Target:** 5 sign-ups by end of Day 2

---

## Job 2 — Hit 3 Course Reps (Days 3–5)

**Who to target:** Final year course reps (400L / 500L) — Engineering, Sciences, Social Sciences, Management Sciences at UNILAG  
**Access:** In person or WhatsApp DM — you're on campus, use it

**Pitch:**
> "I built an AI tool that helps final year students prep for their defense. I'll give you free full access — try it yourself first. If you like it, share it in your class group and I'll give your whole class a discount. No pressure."

**What you're offering each rep:**
- Free Express Defence access for themselves
- A discount code / deal for their class (e.g. ₦200 off, or just a goodwill message)

**After they agree:**
- Grant them Express Defence from admin
- Give them this swipe copy for their WhatsApp group (anonymous — no mention of you):

> "Guys I found this AI tool that simulates a real defense panel — 3 examiners asking you hard questions on your actual project. It's called FYPro. Really useful if you're defending this semester: fypro.com.ng"

**Same copy for your sister and roommates sharing in their department groups.**

**Target:** 3 course reps contacted, at least 1 sharing in their group

---

## Job 3 — First Twitter Thread (Days 4–5)

**Format:** Text only. No face. Just your story.  
**Best time to post:** Thursday or Friday evening (students are on their phones)

**Thread template:**

```
Tweet 1 (hook):
I'm a final year UNILAG student and I watched my classmates 
fail their FYP defense for reasons that had nothing to do 
with their research.

So I built something about it 🧵

Tweet 2 (the problem):
Most students spend months on their project but zero time 
preparing for the defense.

Then they walk in front of 3 examiners and blank out on 
questions they should know cold.

Tweet 3 (what FYPro does):
FYPro puts you in front of 3 AI examiners before the real thing.

The Methodologist. The Subject Expert. The External Examiner.

They ask hard questions. They don't go easy on you. 
That's the point.

Tweet 4 (the result):
If you score 7/10 or above you get a certificate.

But more importantly — you walk into your real defense 
knowing what to expect.

Tweet 5 (CTA):
First 3 questions are free. No payment needed.

If you're defending this semester, try it:
fypro.com.ng

#UNILAG #FinalYearProject #FYP2026 #NigerianStudents
```
